Castle

The main castle is where all the magic happens. The king and queen sleep in the castle, and do many activities such as brewing potions and cooking food. Valuable items such as armor and weapons are also kept here.

"All hail the King!"
Courtyard

The courtyard exists for leisurely purposes. It contains a pool and the beautiful Dragon statue that took weeks to build. It is where guests are brought.

"Beware of the Dragon!"
Basement

The basement is where the first parts of the base were ever built. When the king and queen were in their infancy, they found refuge in the basement and continued to build the majestic palace seen today from there.

"Where it all started."
Animal Farm

The animal farm serves to feed the inhabitants of the castle. It contains pigs, sheep, and cows.

"Oink oink."
Plant Farm

The plant farm produces many vegatables for the inhabitants of Neven World. It contains beets, carrots, wheat, pumpkins and melons.

"The pumpkins and melons are farmed automatically!"
Mines

The mines are a vast network used for mining valuable resources such as gold, iron, and diamonds.

"A lot of time was lost in the mines."
Prison

The prison contains villagers that have been brought into the main base from across Neven World. They now work for Neven.

"They didn't have a choice."
Mob Grinder

The mob grinder is a facility built to spawn enemies, after which they will fall to their death, dropping valuable resources.

"They didn't have a chance."
Throne Room

The throne room is a secret room that only the worthy can enter. It is hidden from plain sight and can only be access by those who know the secret. It contains many valuable resources.

"Kings and their thrones..." {% endblock %} {% block carousel %}
